Many scientists, entrepreneurs, and even artists claim that AI has exploited creativity. However, if you truly understand the underlying principles of these models and how major breakthroughs and trends emerge, you’ll realize that AI can never fully replace creativity. Auto-regressive transformer models are impressive tools, but they represent the collective knowledge of humanity. True creativity, whether in art, science, or business, often begins as an outlier—something AI cannot replicate.
